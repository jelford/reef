class handler.probe.ProbeHandler¶

Bases: handler.dochandler.DocHandler

Handles creation and serving of the probe archive.

GET(request)¶

Get the tgz archive of the probe folder.

The filename of the resulting file will be taken by the browser from the location this handler was attached to.
